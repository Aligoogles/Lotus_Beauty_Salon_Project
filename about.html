About Us

Lotus Beauty Salon was found in 2008 and since then has grown into a major establishment over the last number of years. We have expanded our services adding nails and make up services in 2010 and massages in 2016. We believe that we have become an important member of the Dublin community and by entension, the Irish nation. Out team has been part of major events in people lives such as their wedding day graduation or even just to perk them up.
